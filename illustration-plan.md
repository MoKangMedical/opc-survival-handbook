# OPC生存手册 · 顶级配图方案

## 一、视觉风格体系

### 主风格：《赛博匠人》 Cyber-Craftsman

**设计理念**：将「手工创作的温度」与「AI技术的锐度」融合
- 基础调性：暖灰底 + 深蓝主色 + 琥珀金点缀
- 人物风格：扁平化矢量人物 + 微质感（非纯扁平，非纯拟物）
- 图形语言：圆角几何 + 手绘虚线连接 + 数据可视化元素
- 字体：中文用思源黑体/阿里巴巴普惠体，英文用 Inter

**比色方案**：
- 背景：`#F7F5F2` (暖白)
- 主色：`#1A1C2E` (深海蓝)
- 强调：`#F5A623` (琥珀金)
- 辅助：`#6C9BCF` (天蓝) / `#E8D5B7` (沙色)

## 二、10张章节题图 → AI生成提示词

### 图1：第1章「一个人的时代来了」
```
Prompt: A lone creator sitting at a wooden desk in a minimalist workspace, 
silhouetted against a floor-to-ceiling window showing a futuristic city skyline 
at golden hour. On the desk: one laptop with glowing AI interface. No other people. 
Warm cinematic lighting, 8K photorealistic, Kodak Portra 400 film grain, 
architectural digest photography style. The scale of one person against a giant 
city conveys both vulnerability and immense possibility. 
Aspect 3:4, vertical book illustration.
```

### 图2：第2章「心态——OPC的第一道生死线」
```
Prompt: A climber scaling a steep cliff face at twilight, one hand reaching for 
the next hold. A glowing robotic arm (sleek, Apple-design aesthetic) extends from 
the top of the cliff toward the climber. The rope connects human and AI. 
Dramatic Rim lighting, misty atmosphere below, stars beginning to appear above. 
Stylized realism, mixed media feel — photography base with painterly overlays. 
Mood: determination mixed with vulnerability. 3:4 vertical.
```

### 图3：第3章「找到你的一人帝国」
```
Prompt: Ikigai diagram rendered as four translucent overlapping circles floating 
in a dark cosmic space. Each circle contains symbolic miniature scenes:

"World Needs" circle shows globe and helping hands, "Money" circle shows coins. 
At the center intersection: a glowing geometric crystal labeled "AI Amplification 
Zone". Neon accent lines, cyberpunk-meets-zen aesthetic, ultra-detailed, 
V-Ray render quality, 3:4 vertical.
```
